Lawncare and landscaping


Josh, our landscaping contractor visits weekly, early in the evening, on Thursday or Friday depending on weather.

He's quick, efficient and shouldn't interrupt anything you have planned. If there is a conflict, or you'd prefer he not stop by while you're at the home please let us know. Rescheduling shouldn't be an obstacle.

Trash removal


GFL comes Wednesday (Thursday if a holiday occurs Monday-Wednesday) between 7A and Noon. We usually put it out Tuesday night to avoid missing pickup. If you have trash to dispose of, put it in the large rolling trash can outside garage. Roll it out to the road on east side of driveway. Once emptied, return to original location by garage.

There should be plenty of 42-gallon trash bags on "ladder" to garage attic. If you end up running low/out of bags, let us know in the app and another box will appear like magic.

Parking

You (and your guests too of course!) are welcome to park anywhere in the driveway including spots along east property line. When driveway isn't able to accommodate, temporary daytime street parking on either side of driveway on Canton is okay... Just be courteous to neighbors, they're the best!

If you've made the trip in your Tesla, a Wall Connector is installed in the garage that you're welcome to use.

NOTE: Only foot traffic on path to river. Our golf cart path isn't able to support the weight of passenger vehicles and repairs are expensive.
