Houseplants

Even if they tell you otherwise (these plants can be sneaky) our plantcare contractor suggests not watering the plants. She visits at regular intervals to keep each varity happy, fed, and watered appropriately.

From time to time there may be a "mishap" with one of the plants indoors. Please do your best to place any soil back into pot and let us know as soon as you're able... We'll get a call into the plant lady stat!
