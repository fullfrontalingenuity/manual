Check-in and check-out

Check-in

Official check-in time is 4:00PM Eastern. You're reading this, so entry must've gone without a hitch, excellent! As for specific check-in instructions - there aren't any... We just ask that you message in the app to let us know you've arrived.

To keep this home manual concise we chose to include only the most frequent questions and items you might need out of the gate. If you need a hand with something not in the home manual don't hesitate to ask in the app!

Check-out

Official check-out time is 10:00AM Eastern. If you need more time, please let us know as far in advance as you can. We'll see what we can do to accommodate.

NOTE: Summer rentals are usually back-to-back. It would be challenging to adjust for late check-out.
