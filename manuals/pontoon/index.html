Pontoon (if rented)

If you've opted to rent pontoon boat, keys will be waiting for you on counter next to stove.

Between use and when finished for the week, it should be tied to dock in the same way (similar amount of slack on line, position, etc) and properly secured. Life vests are stored in garage. Please store them in garage between use (vests tend to blow away) and put them back for the next guests.

NOTE: Children under 16 should not drive the boat even with adult(s) present.
