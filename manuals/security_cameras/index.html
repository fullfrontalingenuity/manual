Security cameras

Five *external* security cameras are on-premises. Driveway, front entrance, rear deck, rear porch, river/dock area.

They're only enabled while no guests are checked-in. Once you've checked-in, all security devices are all disabled for the duration of your stay.

NOTE: Please do not remove/relocate cameras... They'll be enabled once you checkout and their placement is important.
