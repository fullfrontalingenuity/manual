Fireplace

Summer rentals

Long story short - our fireplace is inoperable during summer months. It conflicts with the smart thermostat, which ends up shutting cooling off to all rooms but living room and blasting air-conditining to compete.

If you booked a summer rental and fireplace was the deciding factor - let's chat and do a rundown of how it can be made to work.

Winter rentals

Mode and ambient temperature setting is controlled by Siri. For wintertime rentals, you'll find a laminated instruction sheet on mantle. If/when questions arise or something doesn't work quite as expected, just message in the app - we're happy to walk you through it and can even assist remotely.
