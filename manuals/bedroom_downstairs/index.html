Downstairs bedroom and bathroom

Our favorite room!

Desk

If your laptop has a USB-C port, you can use monitors and keyboard/mouse. Otherwise, please leave everything connected and use the desk as-is.

Makeup nook

The left bin is for dirty clothing. Bin at far right is trash. It helps us know what to do with contents if each bin is used as intended.

Towels

Additional towels are in the bathroom closet to right of shower. Used towels should be placed in the washing machine upstairs.

Sheets

If you need to swap sheets for some reason during your stay, there are fresh sheets in bathroom closet. Feel free to leave all sheets in a heap at foot of the bed when you check out.

NOTE: The glass sphere with pump handle contains mouthwash. Red cups are new and intended for you to use for duration of your stay.
