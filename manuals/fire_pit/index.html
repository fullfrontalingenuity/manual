Fire pit

For the moment, we provide wood at no charge. We ask that you limit burning of wood to 20-25 pieces per 6-night stay.

You should find plenty of kindling in a metal bucket (inside) between garage doors. If you run low or out of kindling, there's an aparatus in garage (and small sledge hammer) to split additional wood into kindling-size pieces. Just say the word if you're not comfortable using it or have questions on use.

NOTE: Children under 12 should *not* start fires without close supervision or be left alone near a burning fire due to safety concerns.
