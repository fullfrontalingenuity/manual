Grill

It's on a small platform next to fire pit. When finished grilling, don't forget to clean it. We've found it's easiest to clean while still hot.

You'll find a spare tank in the garage near overhead doors... If you run out of propane, swap out the tanks and we'll take care of the rest after you've departed.

NOTE: Children under 12 should *not* use the grill due to safety concerns.
