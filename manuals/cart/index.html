Golf cart (with signed waiver)

Due to insurance limitations, a signed waiver must be in-place before using the cart. If you or anyone in your party drives the cart, a signed waiver is required for each potential driver. Don't use the cart unless waiver has already been signed and submitted.

Brakes must be used descending the hill, there's a fairly dangerous corner that will be obvious. Keys should be left in ignition switch. When done for the day, please store cart in garage at night and don't park it next to fire pit. You'd be surprised to know why that's mentioned here.

NOTE: Only adults 18 years or older, with a signed and submittd waiver may drive the cart.
