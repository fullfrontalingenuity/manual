Golf cart (if rented)

If you've opted to rent the golf cart, keys will be waiting for you on counter next to stove. It's in the garage and gassed-up!

When done for the day, please store cart in garage at night and don't park it next to fire pit. You'd be surprised to know why that's note was mentioned here.

NOTE: Please, children under 12 should not drive cart without an adult riding along.
